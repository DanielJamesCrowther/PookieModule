About Smart Fish Module

Our idea is a smart fish tank module that is an attachment to be implemented onto any fish tank. It has automatic feeders and sensors to reduce user labour and errors. This solution will help keep the fish healthy and, in the future, could potentially be extended for use in commercial fish farms.

What Does POOKIES stand for?

POOKIE stands for PORTABLE OBJECT ORIENTED KIT for INTERNET of EVERYTHING SOLUTIONS

Components

The solution for our challenge is to automate many of the tasks to care for a fish. Listed below are the key components of the Fish Module

  • Water Flow Sensor

    Monitors water flow rate.

  • Photosensor

    Measures light intensity.

  • Turbidity Sensor

    Detects water clarity.

  • Water Level Sensor

    Monitors water level.

  • Temperature Sensor

    Measures water temperature.

  • ESP32 Microcontroller

    Controls and manages data.
